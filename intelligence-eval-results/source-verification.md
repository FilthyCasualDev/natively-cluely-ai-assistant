### Synthesis Statement

All resume and JD content in the fixture sets was **synthesized from structural/occupational knowledge** and does not contain real personal data. No real person's resume was copied. Names, companies, projects, and salary figures are entirely fictional.

Sources were used as structural inspiration (what sections a resume has, what skills a role requires) rather than content copies.
